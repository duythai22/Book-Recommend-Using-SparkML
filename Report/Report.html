Cch thc hot ng ca phng php Content-based thng bao gm cc bc sau:

  • Xc nh cc c trng (features) ca cc mc: y c th l cc thuc tnh hoc thng tin m t ca cc mc, v d: t kha, th loi, din vin, nhn hiu, v.v.

  • Biu din cc mc di dng vector: Cc c trng ca cc mc c biu din di dng vector.

  • Gi cc mc tng t: Da trn m hnh tng ng, cc mc c mc tng ng cao vi mc ang xem xt c gi cho ngi dng.

def cosineSimilarity(vector1, vector2):
    numerator = np.dot(vector1, vector2)
    denominator = np.sqrt(np.dot(vector1, vector1)) * \
        np.sqrt(np.dot(vector2, vector2))

    return float(numerator / denominator)

def keyWordsRecommendation(keyword, all_books_tfidf):
    input_word_df = sc.parallelize([(0, keyword)]).toDF(["ISBN", "Summary"])
    input_word_df = pipeline_model.transform(input_word_df)
    input_word_tfidf = input_word_df.select("tfidf_vec").collect()[0][0]

    similar_books_rdd = all_books_tfidf.map(
        lambda x: (x[0], cosineSimilarity(x[1], input_word_tfidf))
    )

    sim_book_by_keyword = (
        similar_books_rdd.toDF(["ISBN", "similarity_score"])
        .dropna()
        .orderBy("similarity_score", ascending=False)
        .limit(10)
    )

    return sim_book_by_keyword

5.2 Thut ton ALS

Vi thut ton ny, c mt s siu tham s c s dng cu hnh v hun luyn m hnh ALS. Di y l gii thch cho mi siu tham s:

  • maxIter: y l s lng lp li ti a ca thut ton ALS. N xc nh s ln m hnh s c cp nht theo th t lp li ma trn ngi dng-latent factors v ma trn latent factors-mc.

  • regParam: Tham s iu chun (regularization parameter) c s dng kim sot overfitting trong qu trnh hun luyn. Gi tr regParam cng cao, cng gim kh nng m hnh b overfitting.

  • rank: y l kch thc ca cc latent factors, tc l s chiu ca ma trn ngi dng-latent factors v ma trn latent factors-mc. Thng thng, rank c la chn nh mt gi tr nh hn kch thc thc s ca ma trn nh gi gim chiu d liu v tng tc tnh ton.

Gi tr RMSE nh gi cht lng ca m hnh ALS. RMSE o lng mc sai lch gia gi tr d on v gi tr thc t, v gi tr cng thp th m hnh cng chnh xc.

  • RMSE = sqrt((Gi tr d on - Gi tr thc t)^2 / S lng items)

Thc hin vic tm kim v chn m hnh ALS tt nht da trn cross-validation trn tp d liu hun luyn, v sau in ra cc gi tr tt nht ca cc siu tham s.

5.3 Phng php Content-Based Filtering

Mt pipeline c s dng xy dng mt quy trnh x l d liu vn bn. Di y l gii thch cho mi siu tham s trong quy trnh x l d liu:

  • RegexTokenizer: y l mt b phn mnh (tokenizer) da trn biu thc chnh quy (regex) c s dng phn tch vn bn thnh cc t ring bit. Tham s pattern xc nh biu thc chnh quy tch t, v inputCol l tn ct cha vn bn u vo. Kt qu ca b phn mnh s c lu vo ct tokens.

  • StopWordsRemover: y l mt b loi b t dng (stop words) c s dng loi b cc t ph bin v khng mang nhiu ngha t vn bn. Tham s inputCol l tn ct cha danh sch cc t c phn mnh, v outputCol l tn ct cha kt qu sau khi loi b t dng. Kt qu s c lu vo ct nostopwords.

  • CountVectorizer: y l mt b vector ha m (count vectorizer) c s dng bin i cc vn bn thnh vect m. Tham s inputCol l tn ct cha danh sch cc t c loi b t dng, v outputCol l tn ct cha vect m. Tham s vocabSize xc nh s lng t duy nht trong t in ca vect m.

  • IDF: y l mt b trng s IDF (Inverse Document Frequency) c s dng tnh ton trng s IDF cho cc t trong vect m. Tham s inputCol l tn ct cha vect m, v outputCol l tn ct cha vect trng s IDF.

  • Pipeline: y l mt chui cc giai on (stages) x l d liu vn bn. Cc giai on c xc nh bng danh sch cc i tng x l d liu, bao gm tokenizer, stopWordsRemover, countVectorizer v tfiDF. Cc giai on s c thc hin theo th t trong danh sch.

Sau khi khi to pipeline v nh ngha cc giai on, m hnh pipeline c hun luyn bng cch gi phng thc fit() trn d liu vn bn text_by_book_df. Qu trnh hun luyn s thc hin ln lt cc giai on trong pipeline, v kt qu cui cng l mt pipeline_model cha cc thng tin v trng thi ca m hnh c hun luyn.
